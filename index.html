ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Jake!

I'm a seasoned musician with over two decades of experience in the world of music. Based in the vibrant city of San Diego, I have immersed myself in the art of sound, both as a performer and an educator.

Musical Journey:

My musical journey spans over 20 years, during which I've honed my skills and explored various facets of the industry. I proudly contribute my talents to two exceptional projects — Fulton Lee and Love on the Weekend—where I find joy in creating and sharing music with a wider audience.

Teaching Expertise:

As a dedicated music educator, I specialize in teaching guitar and music production using Ableton Live. I also teach piano, bass, and drums. Guiding aspiring musicians on their own unique musical paths is a fulfilling aspect of my professional life. I believe in nurturing creativity and fostering a deep appreciation for the art of music.

Crafting Sound:

Beyond performance and education, I am a passionate beginner luthier. I find immense satisfaction in the intricate process of crafting and fine-tuning guitars, ensuring each instrument produces a harmonious and exceptional sound. Additionally, I offer expert guitar repair and setup services to fellow musicians.
